The Altitude Experience

The luxurious experience of dining at Altitude begins before you even enter the restaurant. Guests are greeted curbside by our valet, and a walk down a red carpet leads into the restaurant. Once inside, lavish six-foot chandeliers draw the eye towards the ceiling, and the leather-embossed walls display classically inspired artwork and black and white family photographs. Marble embossed floors provide an elegant touch to the decor.
